About Us

Kumar Food Industries LTD.

Kumar Food Industries Ltd (KFIL) is the most reputed name in the field of food products. The Company brand is India's fastest selling wheat flour and has made a popular name in the global market. The distinctiveness of products and further broadening of product lines has established the company as the most acknowledged manufacturer, exporter and supplier of Wheat Flour, Basmati and Non-Basmati Rice, Pulses and Beans, Corn Flour, Maize Flour, Atta, Mustard Oil, Pickles and Samoline (Suji)

Brief History

  • M/s Kumar Food Industries Ltd is engaged in manufacturing /grinding of Chana Dal & Besan. The manufacturing unit is at Siraspur (Delhi). The Company is utilizing capacity at reasonable level and making maximum utilization of available resources. Kumar Food Industries Ltd products are a familiar household name that spells quality and trust across millions worldwide and also marketed by our company. Its leadership qualities in Indian Food Segment are driven by its availability, quality and consumer awareness. Ours mission and vision is to be able to reach every household across the globe with its Quality Produce for a healthy lifestyle.
  • KFIL has its own manufacturing plant at Delhi to manufacture Besan, Atta, Tea blending & allied product range and now tie up with the processing mills in Delhi and other cities for aforesaid products – i.e. Atta, Besan, etc and new range of product Namkeen, Chips, Snacks, Grocery and related items. Further the items will be increased over a period of time. Therefore also, the company has started its own marketing of the product range. It is thought to flourish and established itself as brand by the company through incur of some reasonable expenditures on advertisement and publicity, accordingly the benefit of the same can be used in the business model.

Distribution Network

KFIL has a wide and strong distribution network with presence in PAN India from Kashmir to Kanyakumari comprising of 2500+ strong distributors/ dealers in all major centers of the country. As the company will expand, the Company propose to promotes these dealers into distributor and distributor into Super Stockiest so that they also reap the benefit of the expansion of the Group. Backed by the strength of wide distribution reach and high brand equity, KFIL has been able to successfully enter into other categories of food products like Rice, Salt, Tea, Pickles to name a few.

Key Highlights

  • Shakti Bhog Foods Limited is 19 year old Company, manufacturing and selling food items like Wheat flour, Dalia, Gram Flour, Maida etc. under the established and well known brand name of 'Shakti Bhog'
  • The Company has over the years established itself as a pioneer in the country for branded daily use food items like wheat flour, Suji etc
  • In addition to its own manufacturing facilities catering mainly to North India, the company has third party tieups in other regions of the country for the manufacture and supply of its products under its brand name
  • It has a well established retail marketing network including tie-ups with major retail chains including Big Bazaar and Reliance for the sale of its products
  • As a diversification measure, the Company in FY 2000 entered into the area of rice manufacturing from raw rice and has over the years established itself as Basmati rice exporter. Subsequently, as a backward integration measure, it started processing of paddy through tie up basis to get maximum benefits in terms of quality and price for its rice sales
  • In FY'11, it acquired the rice mill of Tilda Rice Land Pvt. Ltd. at Karnal (Haryana) to take advantage of the experience gained in the rice manufacturing
  • Last Year, the Company has established a Biscuits and Cookies manufacturing unit at Haridwar with the products being marketed under the brand name “DIVSS”. Its Biscuits and Cookies have been well received in the market
  • In the current year, Company is setting up a new Wheat Flour manufacturing unit at 33, SSI Industrial Estate, Karnal Road, Delhi, with an installed capacity of 1200000 MT p.a.
  • SBFL is also in the process of intensifying sale of Tea under its brand name for which they have already entered into tie-ups at Assam and Darjeeling
